Senior Capstone Project

Senior Spring

16.84 Advanced Autonomous Robotic Systems: The thread's capstone class, where seniors design and build their own autonomous project. The class is very open ended, and students choose a different project each year, be it delivery systems, guides, or something new. This year, students are focusing on drone-related challenges, and are designing a system that automatically inspects infrastructure.
